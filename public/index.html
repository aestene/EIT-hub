Subsea Shuttle Prototype

Press one of the buttons below to choose
what to control the shuttle with.

GAMEPAD: Left joystick controls forward/backward
(up/down) and translation (left/right).
Right joystick controls up/down (up/down)
and rotation (left/right).

Choose Flight Mode of the shuttle by pressing one of
the buttons shown below on your gamepad controller.
You can also choose to arm or disarm the shuttle.
MANUAL STABILIZE
DEPTH HOLD nothing yet

Disarm shuttle Arm shuttle

KEYBOARD: Keys W/S controls forward/backward
and keys A/D translates left/right.
↑ ↓ controls up/down and ← → rotates left/right.
