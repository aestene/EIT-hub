Subsea Shuttle Prototype

Press one of the buttons below to choose what to
control the shuttle with.

GAMEPAD: Left joystick controls forward/backward (up/down) and
translation (left/right). Right joystick controls up/down (up/down)
and rotation (left/right).

KEYBOARD: W/S controls forward/backward and
A/D translates left/right. ↑ ↓ controls up/down
and ← → rotates left/right.
